# Lisp

Life as an engineer was fun, life as a lisp hacker was fun, life as a CTO was fun, life as a founder/CEO was fun. And now life as an investor is fun.

Why? Because I [know](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bmHTFo2Rf2w) how to [beat the averages](http://www.paulgraham.com/avg.html). I won’t bother with more words on that topic, but I have used Clojure across 3 companies over the past 12 years that have created billions of dollars of value. We were among the first Clojure production systems in the world with Runa, and I started the first and [world’s largest Clojure user group](https://www.meetup.com/The-Bay-Area-Clojure-User-Group), and wrote one of the [earliest books](https://www.amazon.com/Clojure-Action-Elegant-Applications-JVM/dp/1935182595/) in the ecosystem. After Runa, was [Quintype](https://quintype.com/), another massive AI/media platform powered by Clojure.

I am deeply thankful to the entire Clojure community, and I have tried to give back in small ways over the years. [Awake](https://awake.vc/) is another way.

As for private equity and [VC](https://coventurecap.com/), I always say – first build an AI to do your job.

We’re investing in [Shoptype](https://shoptype.com/), the ultimate leveler.

Powered by a [LISP](Lisp%20bf8d238cb8444a9c9f903384bee585bd.md) core, this kernel abstracts the workings of the World Wide Web and online marketplaces, creating a new high speed platform for global *social commerce*. The Shoptype AI already powers many billions of global page views and social interactions across dozens of media brands, and now a growing number of [D2C brands](https://shoptype.com/portfolio).

Awake is a new type of platform builder, one that is *functional* for everyone. (Bad pun, I know). And everyone can build on this new Internet, this is the [revenge](http://www.paulgraham.com/icad.html) engine.
