# PE

How to build Berkshire Hathaway 2.0? What is Private Equity and why don't people like PE types?

This question is not dissimilar from how to build Amazon if it were to be built from scratch today. Answer: not as a monolithic company but as an [Ecosystems](Ecosystems%20fa1c17f5980440a99f60eb80339acdb7.md)  of interconnected companies. All the common technology would still be exposed as Amazon Web Services. A venture group would invest into companies built on top of their platforms. Rinse-and-repeat.

![Awake A64Z (3).jpg](PE/Awake_A64Z_(3).jpg)

In today's world, things are a bit more complex with multiple [Platform](Platform%2068204b2b7c004925a4e2152a7d28a3f1.md)  companies and multiple [Blockchain](Blockchain%2065674aea130244e6953c486b9e611641.md) companies that need to be integrated. So a *tech-enabled* private equity approach might be beneficial, particularly if an *integration-first* approach were to be coupled with a *cash-flows-first* approach on the business side.

**Aka PE funds would not just bring capital but also a tech-first integration-focused playbook.** 

Awake has built a technology platform for private equity investment companies for increasing return on assets by leveraging [Connections](Connections%20a73d9b3f36604e6192e1e4085367a96f.md)  between everything and everyone and driving up efficiency as well as agility by creating an entirely digital and indeed virtual enterprise powered by FinTech and AI.
