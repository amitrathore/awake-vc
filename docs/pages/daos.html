DAOs

Organizations of the future are not going to look like today's hierarchical control mechanisms, but cooperative networks filled with members that belong to multiple collectives.

DAO types

A Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) is essentially a blockchain-powered smart-contract-driven platform that empowers a community of people to work together and co-create value in a transparent manner.

While DAOs are a great idea, it will be a long time before they are run entirely by AI, and until then, they're really hybrid and combine regular aspects of organizations with operations decentralized to various degrees.

ORG types

Ultimately, the DAO is a blockchain-based representation of the organization in the real-world. The people dynamics of the workplace are what need to change and adapt to this new world of tech-enabled innovation networks.

Businesses that embrace this new type of organization structure - the simple network structure found in nature - are the ones that are going to win across innovations and agility.
